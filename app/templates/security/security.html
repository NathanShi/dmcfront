SECURITY @ DIGITAL MANUFACTURING COMMONS

DMC’s Security Approach DMC is a suite of software and services with three components; an open architecture software Platform, an apps Marketplace and Professional Services. We are committed to our security practices and helping you understand our approach.

DMC: Holistic Approach

DMC is leading the industry in our security approach to protect digital manufacturing. We have adopted NIST 800-53 and ITAR frameworks improving security controls above the industry standards.

  • People – Our team of professionals have to meet internal requirements, policies and standards
  • Security and privacy training – the organization undergoes annual training, DMC team members have a monthly security training session which covers: privacy, security topics (including device security), acceptable use, preventing malware, physical security, data privacy, account management and incident reporting. Employees are informed that failure to comply will result in consequences, up to and including termination.

DMC: Dedicated Security Practices

DMC has defined roles and job responsibilities dealing with all aspects of securing the environment, platform and organization.

The security program covers:

  • Establishing secure development practices and standards
  • Security and privacy training – the organization undergoes annual training, DMC team members have a monthly security training session which covers: privacy, security topics (including device security), acceptable use, preventing malware, physical security, data privacy, account management and incident reporting. Employees are informed that failure to comply will result in consequences, up to and including termination.
