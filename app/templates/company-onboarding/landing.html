DMDII Membership Tier Descriptions

The Digital Manufacturing and Design Innovation Institute is able to offer Industry, Academic, and Government institutions membership at multiple tiers. See what the institue can offer to you below.

All Industry Members Get access to:

  • Access to Members-Only networking database
  • Invitation to quarterly Members-Only Technology Showcases, including technology demonstrations and curated start-up showcases
  • Development of and participation in Enterprise Projects – multi-party, government cost match projects
  • Contract research & development using DMDII’s Manufacturing Lab
  • Access to a Standard Agreement to facilitate multi-party collaboration

Tier 1

$400,000 Per Company/Year

Members may be invited to:

  • Showcase their technology in DMDII’s Manufacturing Lab

Members also get access to:

  • License and usage of Project IP on enterprise projects
  • Participation in design, optimization and operation of DMDII Future Factory Initiative
  • Guaranteed invitation to quarterly Workshops focused on specific digital manufacturing priority topics

UI LABS Facilty:

  • Facility usage, including executive, conference and individual rooms 8 day/year

Members get to lead:

  • Develop and Participate in multi-party and member funded Partner Innovation Projects
  • Executive Committee, which is responsible for DMDII governance
  • A Technical Committee Seat, which is responsible for guiding the technical direction of DMDII including the strategic investment plan
